KonoSuba - God's blessing on this wonderful world! 3 Anime's 2nd Promo Video Reveals Theme Songs, April 10 Premiere

Machico performs opening theme, 3 cast members perform ending theme

Trương Xuân Huy

On Friday, Kadokawa released the second promotional video for KonoSuba - God's blessing on this wonderful world! 3, the third season of the main KonoSuba – God’s blessing on this wonderful world!! anime. The video unveiled the premiere date of April 10 and introduced Machico as the singer of the opening theme "Growing Up," while the main cast members Sora Amamiya, Rie Takahashi, and Ai Kayano will perform the ending theme "Ano Hi no Mama no Bokura" (Us, Just Like on That Day).

The anime is set to air on April 10 and will be broadcasted on Tokyo MX, BS11, KBS Kyoto, Sun TV, and TV Aichi. Crunchyroll will simultaneously stream the anime as it airs.

New cast additions for this season include:

  • Kanon Takao as Iris
  • Sayuri Yahagi as Claire
  • Reina Ueda as Rain

Teaser visual

Takaomi Kanasaki, director of the previous seasons, will serve as chief director for the third season at Drive. Yujiro Abe, known for his work on series like Kaguya-sama: Love is War and Great Pretender, directs the series. Makoto Uezu returns for the series scripts, Koichi Kikuta as the character designer, and Masato Kōda as the composer.

The first main season premiered in January 2016, followed by the second in January 2017, both streamed by Crunchyroll. Additionally, the KONOSUBA - God's Blessing on This Wonderful World– Legend of Crimson film debuted in Japan in August 2019, later screened by Crunchyroll and Fathom Events in the United States in November 2019.

The television adaptation of Natsume Akatsuki and Kurone Mishima's spinoff novel series, Konosuba: An Explosion on This Wonderful World!, aired 12 episodes starting in April 2023.
