Fate/Grand Order -Epic of Remnant- Ashu Tokuiten IV Kinki Kōrin Teien Salem: Itan Naru Salem Manga Starts Final Arc

Aoi Ohmori launched series based on game in January 2019

Trương Xuân Huy

The 58th chapter of Aoi Ohmori's manga, Fate/Grand Order -Epic of Remnant- Ashu Tokuiten IV Kinki Kōrin Teien Salem: Itan Naru Salem (Fate/Grand Order -Epic of Remnant- Subspecies Singularity IV: Taboo Advent Salem: Salem of Heresy), was published in the April issue of Ichijinsha's Monthly Comic Rex magazine on Tuesday, marking the beginning of the series' final arc.

Adapting the fourth part of the Fate/Grand Order smartphone game's "Epic of Remnant" storyline, Ohmori started the manga serialization in Monthly Comic Rex in January 2019. The seventh compiled volume was released by Ichijinsha on the same day.

Teaser visual

The event first debuted in the Japanese version of the game back in November 2017.

Trailer

Aniplex initially launched the Fate/Grand Order smartphone game in Japan during the summer of 2015. It later received an English release in the United States and Canada in June 2017.

Source: Monthly Comic Rex April issue
