Yuu Tanaka's Reincarnated as a Sword Webnovel Ends

Series launched in September 2015

Trương Xuân Huy

The final chapter of Yuu Tanaka's Reincarnated as a Sword webnovel was published on the Shōsetsuka ni Narō website. The novels, launched in September 2015, are illustrated by LLO and published by Gentosha. The 17th volume is set to release on March 29 in Japan. Tomowo Maruyama adapted the series into a manga on Gentosha's Denshi Birz website, with the 15th volume releasing on March 23.

Seven Seas Entertainment is releasing both the light novels and manga in English. The manga follows the story of a magical sword reincarnated with memories of his past life and a young beastgirl named Fran. Together, they embark on a journey filled with adventure and discovery.

Hinako Inoue created the Reincarnated as a Sword: Another Wish manga spinoff, which concluded in March 2023. Seven Seas Entertainment is also releasing this manga in English.

Teaser visual

The original novels' anime adaptation premiered in October 2022 and will have a second season.
