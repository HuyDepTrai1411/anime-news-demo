Bartender Glass of God TV Anime Casts Naoya Uchida

Trailer reveals returning cast from Duel Masters Win anime

Trương Xuân Huy

The official website for Bartender Glass of God (Bartender: Kami no Glass), the upcoming television anime adaptation of Araki Joh and Kenji Nagatomo's Bartender manga, announced on Friday that Naoya Uchida will voice Ryūchi Kuzuhara, also known as "Mr. Perfect" and the bartender of "K" at Hotel Diamond Star. The website also revealed a visual featuring Ryūchi and a Manhattan cocktail.

Last week, the website disclosed that Hiroki Tōchi will portray Kitakata.

The anime is scheduled to premiere in April 2024, and Crunchyroll will stream it in various regions, including North America, Europe, and Oceania.

The main cast includes:

  • Takuma Terashima as Ryū Sasakura
  • Yoshino Nanjō as Miwa Kurushima
  • Haruka Shiraishi as Yukari Higuchi
  • Eriko Matsui as Kyoko Kawakami
  • Ryoko Shiraishi as Yuri Kinjo
  • Makoto Furukawa as Kelvin Chen

Teaser visual

Ryōichi Kuraya is directing the anime at studio Liber, with Mariko Kunisawa overseeing the series scripts and Yōichi Ueda serving as the character designer and chief animation director. SUNTORY is cooperating on the production.

A previous 11-episode television anime adaptation of the manga premiered in Japan in October 2006. Masaki Watanabe directed the series at Palm Studio, with Yasuhiro Imagawa handling the scripts and series composition. Shout! Factory and Anime Limited released the first anime on Blu-ray Disc in January 2021.

Trailer

Shueisha serialized the original manga in its Super Jump magazine starting in 2004 before moving it to Grand Jump. The manga concluded with its 21st volume in February 2012. Additionally, the manga inspired a live-action television adaptation that debuted in Japan in January 2011.

Source: Bartender Glass of God anime's website
