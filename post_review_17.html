Dog Signal

ドッグシグナル

Trương Xuân Huy

Ranked: #17

Type: TV Serie

Status: Currently Airing

Aired: Oct 22, 2023 to Mar 17, 2024

Episodes: 20

Premiered: Winter 2023

Synopsis: After his girlfriend Yuuko breaks up with him, Miyu Samura's luck hits rock bottom. But months later, Yuuko reappears and gives Miyu a toy poodle she can't care for. With no dog knowledge, Miyu struggles with the rowdy pup, risking eviction.

During a walk, Miyu meets professional dog trainer Shinichirou Niwa and his well-behaved poodle Ourson. Impressed by Niwa's skills, Miyu quits his job to become his assistant. However, Niwa struggles with human interactions, and his business suffers.

Despite challenges, Miyu remains determined to support Niwa and his failing business.

As others have pointed out, Dog Signal is very underrated. While the animation may not be outstanding, the anime excels in its overall story, well-researched information, emotionally-driven plot lines, and compelling main characters.

For dog lovers like myself, the accurate and entertaining portrayal of dog behavior and training in this anime is impressive. Each episode delves into the complexities of a dog's psyche, offering valuable insights and reflections for viewers.

Teaser visual

One of the charms of Dog Signal is its puzzle-solving aspect, as viewers are encouraged to analyze the root causes of the dogs' behaviors, often stemming from human actions or misunderstandings.

The realistic portrayal of different dog breeds and their behaviors adds to the authenticity of the series and reflects positively on the writing team's efforts.

The pacing of each episode is well-balanced, with satisfying resolutions to the issues presented. The main character, Miyu, undergoes development and maturity throughout the series, making him relatable and endearing.

Trailer

While the music may not be memorable, the opening theme is pleasant. Miyu's occasional loudness is balanced by his growth and dedication to the animals, particularly highlighted in episode 12.

Overall, Dog Signal is a hidden gem for dog lovers and offers valuable insights into dog behavior and training, wrapped in engaging storytelling and characters.
