Shouwa Genroku Rakugo Shinjuu: Sukeroku Futatabi-hen

Descending Stories: Showa Genroku Rakugo Shinju

Trương Xuân Huy

Ranked: #12

Type: TV Serie

Status: Finished Airing

Aired: Jan 7, 2017 to Mar 25, 2017

Episodes: 12

Premiered: Winter 2017

Synopsis: Despite achieving the highest rank of shin'uchi in rakugo, Yotarou grapples with his identity in the art form. Struggling to balance his master's teachings with the influence of the late Sukeroku, Yotarou's performances lack the crucial element of ego. While he draws crowds to theaters, rakugo itself faces the threat of fading into obscurity.

Meanwhile, Yakumo, revered as the stalwart defender of rakugo's tradition, wrestles with the challenges of old age. Though his performances remain impeccable, he fears he is reaching his limits, particularly as an old friend reemerges in his life. Konatsu, facing the responsibilities of single motherhood, clashes with Yotarou's offer to marry her and raise her son together.

Shouwa Genroku Rakugo Shinjuu: Sukeroku Futatabi-hen is a profound homage to the art of rakugo, weaving a devastating character drama across generations. It captivates with its exploration of human relationships and the burden of love, whether for art, family, or friendship.

The series masterfully integrates elements of rakugo into its storytelling, creating a rich and complex narrative. Each episode delves into a different youkai encounter, ultimately exploring the bonds between humans and youkai, as well as the bonds among humans themselves.

Teaser visual

The meticulous attention to detail is evident throughout the series, from the purposeful imagery in the opening sequences to the subtle foreshadowing of future events. Despite its seemingly static animation, the show's minimalist presentation enhances its complexity, complementing the core idea of rakugo.

The voice acting is exceptional, with each actor skillfully portraying the emotional depth of their characters. Characters evolve dynamically, influenced by minor characters like Higuchi and Matsuda, whose presence adds depth to the narrative.

Shouwa Genroku Rakugo Shinjuu: Sukeroku Futatabi-hen is a testament to the enduring power of storytelling. It explores themes of change, legacy, and the passage of time, ultimately asserting that death is not the end but a beginning—a rebirth.

Trailer

The show's brilliance is overwhelming, leaving a lasting impact on viewers and challenging them to contemplate its profound ideas. It is a journey that resonates deeply, forever etching itself onto the soul of those who experience it.

Thank you to everyone involved in creating this remarkable masterpiece.
