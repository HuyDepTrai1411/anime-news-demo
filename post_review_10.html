Servamp

オレンジ

Trương Xuân Huy

Ranked: #10

Type: TV Serie

Status: Finished Airing

Aired: Jul 5, 2016 to Sep 20, 2016

Episodes: 12

Premiered: Summer 2016

Synopsis: Mahiru Shirota believes in simplicity and avoiding trouble whenever possible. However, his life takes an unexpected turn when he rescues a stray cat and names it Kuro. Little does Mahiru know, Kuro is actually a lazy shut-in vampire.

One day, Mahiru discovers a mysterious young man in his home who turns out to be Kuro in human form. Despite Mahiru's initial panic, he forms a contract with Kuro, inadvertently involving himself in a dangerous world of supernatural battles and bloodthirsty creatures.

As a collector and reader of the manga series, I was excited for the anime adaptation, but I would now recommend reading the manga first. While the adaptation captures some elements well, there are notable changes in the plot sequence, and some original storylines are omitted or shortened.

Teaser visual

The art and effects, especially during fight scenes, are well done, incorporating the mangaka's original style effectively. However, the animation in comedy scenes lacks fluidity and fails to convey the comedic aspect adequately.

The background music could have been better, but the theme songs effectively set the mood for the show. The voice actors perform admirably, though some characters receive more development than others.

Trailer

The friendship theme is prevalent in the plot, which isn't necessarily a bad thing, but it dominates the series. The action is enjoyable, but the dramatic scenes could have been improved.

Overall, while I enjoy the adaptation, I feel it falls short of capturing the depth of the manga. However, for fans of dark shoujo and reverse harem genres, the anime offers an appealing cast of characters and an absence of romance that may appeal to some viewers.

Source: myanimelist.net
